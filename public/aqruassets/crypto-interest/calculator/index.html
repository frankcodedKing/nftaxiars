How do I calculate expected earnings from APY?

When it comes to calculating your expected earnings using APY, it’s important to understand how this figure is derived. APY, or annual percentage yield, is a measure of the average interest rate earned on a financial investment over the course of a year. You can then use this figure to calculate the overall expected return on investments.

For example, if you have a Crypto investment of $1,000 and an APY of 10%, your expected earnings would be $100 after a year.

It’s important to note that the APY is only an estimation of returns, and actual profits may vary, due to many factors, including market conditions and the performance of the underlying investment.
